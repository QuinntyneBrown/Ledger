Aurora Foundations Elevation
Foundations

Elevation

Depth in the dark is delicate. Aurora layers a soft ambient shadow with a tighter contact shadow, then adds a faint top highlight so raised surfaces catch light like glass. Reserve glow for the brand and success moments.

Shadow scale

Four levels. Every step pairs a wide, low-opacity ambient shadow with a small contact shadow — the higher the level, the further and softer the object floats.

Level 1--shadow-1 · resting cards, chips
Level 2--shadow-2 · toasts, tooltips, thumbs
Level 3--shadow-3 · popovers, raised cards, FAB
Level 4--shadow-4 · dialogs, sheets, drawers

The top highlight

A 1px inset light line along the top edge, applied with every card shadow. It is what separates an Aurora surface from a flat rectangle — subtle, but the whole product feels crafted because of it.

elevation/highlight-top
No highlight
+ highlight-top

The default .au-card already composes var(--shadow-1), var(--highlight-top). When you set a custom box-shadow, remember to append var(--highlight-top) to keep the surface consistent.

Glow

Coloured light, used sparingly. --shadow-glow-brand haloes the primary button and brand mark; --shadow-glow-good celebrates a positive result — a goal reached, a streak kept.

elevation/glow
Glow is an accent, not an elevation. One glowing element per view. Overuse and the brand light stops meaning "this is the thing to act on".

Glass

.au-card--glass blurs whatever sits behind it (--blur-glass · 18px) and rides on a translucent surface. It is how sticky headers, the app status bar and floating panels stay legible over scrolling content.

elevation/glass
Frosted panel

Backdrop blur keeps text readable over any colour beneath. Used for the sticky doc header and the app status bar.

Choosing a level

Elevation communicates hierarchy: the higher a surface floats, the more it interrupts. Match the level to how much the element should command attention.

TokenLevelUse for
--shadow-1RestingCards, list rows, chips — content that sits in the page flow.
--shadow-2Hover / small floatToasts, tooltips, slider thumbs, segmented active state.
--shadow-3RaisedPopovers, menus, the FAB, cards lifting on hover.
--shadow-4OverlayDialogs, bottom sheets, drawers — modal surfaces above a scrim.
--shadow-glow-brandAccentThe primary button and brand mark only.
--shadow-glow-goodCelebrationMilestones and positive confirmations.
