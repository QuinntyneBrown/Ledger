Aurora Components Dialog
Feedback & navigation

Dialog

A focused, blocking surface that interrupts the flow to ask for one decision. It floats above a blurred scrim, leads with an intent icon, and pairs a clear primary action with an easy way out. The mobile bottom sheet is its thumb-reachable sibling.

Set your goal weight?

We’ll track your progress from today’s 78.4 kg toward 73.0 kg. You can change this any time in settings.

When to use it

Reach for a dialog when the app cannot continue until the person responds — confirming a destructive action, committing an important change, or acknowledging something consequential. Because it steals focus and dims everything behind it, use it sparingly.

  • Use it for a single, well-scoped decision with at most two or three actions.
  • Prefer the bottom sheet on mobile for input-heavy tasks like the quick weigh-in — it stays within thumb reach.
  • For a message that doesn’t block progress, use a snackbar or banner instead.

Anatomy

Dialog title

A short line of supporting body copy that frames the decision being asked for.

1 2 3 4
  • 1 Icon — signals intent: brand, danger or good.
  • 2 Title — the question or statement, phrased plainly.
  • 3 Body — one or two lines of context, no more.
  • 4 Actions — dismiss on the left, the committing action on the right.

Do & don’t

Do
Delete this entry?
Name the consequence in the title and color the destructive action so the stakes are unmistakable.
Don’t
Are you sure?
This will do the thing you asked for.
Don’t use a vague title or crowd the footer with ambiguous, competing choices.

Confirm dialog

A brand-tinted intent icon and a gold primary action. Open it, then dismiss with the button, the close icon, the scrim or Esc.

dialog/confirm
<button class="au-btn au-btn--primary" data-dialog-open="dlg-confirm">Set goal weight</button>

<div class="au-scrim" id="dlg-confirm" hidden>
  <div class="au-dialog" role="dialog" aria-modal="true">
    <button class="au-icon-btn au-dialog-close" data-dialog-close aria-label="Close">
      <svg class="au-icon"><use href="#i-x"/></svg>
    </button>
    <div class="au-dialog-icon"><svg class="au-icon"><use href="#i-flag"/></svg></div>
    <h2 class="au-dialog-title">Set your goal weight?</h2>
    <p class="au-dialog-body">We&rsquo;ll track from 78.4 kg toward 73.0 kg.</p>
    <div class="au-dialog-actions">
      <button class="au-btn au-btn--outlined" data-dialog-close>Not yet</button>
      <button class="au-btn au-btn--primary" data-dialog-close>Set goal</button>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Destructive dialog

The is-danger icon and a solid coral action mark an irreversible choice.

dialog/destructive
<div class="au-scrim" id="dlg-delete" hidden>
  <div class="au-dialog au-dialog--sm" role="dialog" aria-modal="true">
    <div class="au-dialog-icon is-danger"><svg class="au-icon"><use href="#i-trash"/></svg></div>
    <h2 class="au-dialog-title">Delete this weigh-in?</h2>
    <p class="au-dialog-body">The 74.9 kg entry from Jul 18 will be removed. This can&rsquo;t be undone.</p>
    <div class="au-dialog-actions">
      <button class="au-btn au-btn--outlined" data-dialog-close>Cancel</button>
      <button class="au-btn au-btn--danger-solid" data-dialog-close>Delete</button>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Bottom sheet

On mobile, slide a .au-sheet up from the bottom edge. The grip signals it can be dismissed downward; it’s ideal for the quick weigh-in.

dialog/bottom-sheet
<div class="au-sheet-scrim" id="sheet-weighin" hidden>
  <div class="au-sheet" role="dialog" aria-modal="true" aria-label="Log weight">
    <div class="au-sheet-grip"></div>
    <!-- big numeric entry, actions, etc. -->
  </div>
</div>

Sizes

Add --sm for a terse confirm or --lg for a form.

Structure & classes

ClassElementRole
.au-scrimoverlayFixed, blurred backdrop that centers the dialog. Click to dismiss.
.au-dialogsurfaceThe floating panel. Add .au-dialog--sm / .au-dialog--lg.
.au-dialog-icondivIntent chip. Add .is-danger or .is-good.
.au-dialog-titleh2The decision, in one plain line.
.au-dialog-bodypSupporting context.
.au-dialog-actionsdivFooter row; children flex to equal width.
.au-dialog-closebuttonOptional icon button pinned top-right.
.au-sheet-scrim / .au-sheetoverlayBottom-anchored variant with a .au-sheet-grip.

Behaviour

Attribute / APIEffect
data-dialog-open="ID"On a trigger — reveals the overlay with that id.
data-dialog-closeOn any element inside — closes the nearest overlay.
Esc / scrim clickDismisses the open overlay.
Aurora.openDialog(id)Open programmatically; Aurora.closeDialog(id) to close.
Accessibility. Put role="dialog" aria-modal="true" on .au-dialog and point aria-labelledby at the title (or add aria-label on the sheet). Icon-only closers need an aria-label. Focus should move into the dialog on open and return to the trigger on close; entry animations are disabled under prefers-reduced-motion.
