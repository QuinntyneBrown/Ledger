Aurora Components Banner
Feedback & navigation

Banner

A persistent, in-context alert that stays put until it’s resolved. Unlike a snackbar it doesn’t float or fade — it sits inside the layout, tinted by tone, to explain a condition and offer a way to fix it.

Couldn’t save your weigh-in
We’ll keep it on this device and try again automatically.

When to use it

Use a banner for a condition that persists and may need a response — you’re offline, a sync failed, a goal was reached, a trial is ending. Because it lives in the page, it stays visible while the person decides what to do.

  • Place it close to what it affects — the top of a screen for global state, inside a card for local state.
  • Match the tone to the message: info, good, warn or bad. Never dress a routine notice as an error.
  • For a transient receipt, use a snackbar; to block until resolved, a dialog.

Anatomy

Banner title
A line of supporting text that explains the condition.
1 2 3 4
  • 1 Icon — tone marker, colored to match the modifier.
  • 2 Title — the condition, stated in a few words.
  • 3 Text — what it means or what happens next.
  • 4 Action — optional; the fix or the next step.

Do & don’t

Do
You’re offline
State the condition plainly and give one clear action to resolve it.
Don’t
Nice weigh-in!
Don’t mismatch tone and message — a positive note wrapped in error red erodes trust.

Offline notice

A calm warn banner that reassures data isn’t lost, with a single retry.

banner/offline
You’re offline
New weigh-ins are saved here and will sync when you reconnect.

Tones

Four modifiers cover the full range of in-context messaging.

banner/tones
Weekly report is ready
You’re down 0.6 kg over the last 7 days.
Goal reached
You hit 73.0 kg. Set a new goal to keep the momentum.
No entry in 3 days
A quick weigh-in keeps your trend line honest.
Sync failed
We couldn’t reach the server. We’ll retry shortly.

Structure & classes

ClassElementRole
.au-bannerwrapperThe alert row: icon, body and optional trailing action.
.au-banner-bodydivHolds the title and text; flexes to fill.
.au-banner-titledivThe condition, in high-emphasis text.
.au-banner-textdivSupporting detail in muted text.

Tone modifiers

ModifierUse forIcon
.au-banner--infoNeutral notices, tips, reports.#i-info
.au-banner--goodSuccess, goals reached, on-track state.#i-check-circle
.au-banner--warnAttention needed but not broken (offline, stale data).#i-alert-triangle
.au-banner--badErrors and failures the person may need to fix.#i-alert-circle
Accessibility. Give a banner that appears in response to an action role="status" (or role="alert" for errors) so it’s announced. Never encode meaning in color alone — the icon and title carry it too. Actions are standard buttons and keep the built-in focus ring.
