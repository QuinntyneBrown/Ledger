Chart

A calm, data-first trend line built from hand-written SVG — no charting library. It layers a moving-average band, a dashed goal line and milestone annotations so the direction of travel reads instantly.

90-day trend74.9kg
−3.5 kg
Daily 7-day average Range band Goal 73.0 kg

Principles

  • Trend over points. The daily line is quiet; the 7-day average is the confident read.
  • The goal is always visible. A dashed brand line anchors every chart to its target.
  • Annotate meaning, not noise. Mark milestones and events — never every data point.
  • Draw on entry. The line animates in once, then holds still. Disabled under reduced motion.

    <path class="au-chart-band" .../>      <!-- range band -->
    <line class="au-chart-goal" .../>      <!-- goal line -->
    <path class="au-chart-area" .../>      <!-- gradient fill -->
    <path class="au-chart-line au-chart-line--ma" .../>
    <path class="au-chart-line au-chart-draw" .../>   <!-- animates in -->
    <circle class="au-chart-dot" .../>
  </svg>
</div>
